We heard that Scotland is worth visiting. And after several weeks of thinking about it we found that there were no train tickets, so we bought a flight, booked a room in small hotel 70km from Glasgow and a car. Also we had a list of places we were going to see. But finally we had to find another car and visited only one place from our list. The most important thing while driving through Higlands is to have maps cached in your cell phone as there's almost no signal. And remember that nights in the end of August are really cold.
